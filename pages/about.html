About us

Our association, created in 2000 is named "Just for smile". We chose this name because by taking this responsability, by our actions, we want to make people smile. Anyone deserves a life like everyone else, anyone deserves to be happy. So we try to find solutions against a lot of problems in different countries like Kenya, Nigeria and South-Africa for a better education, a better earth and just for a precious smile.
