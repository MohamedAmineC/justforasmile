Just for a smile

Despite all the magic and all the beauty that we can find in Kenya, wildlife is threatened, animals and plants are endangered, local people are threatened, biodiversity is threatened, children are threatened. And with a similar situation, Kenya hurts and is moaning.

So we have made an association to improve our present and to take action for our future, a future with better places for animals, for locals and for us, give just a smile to all kenyans and all the world by changing our way to be and our bad habits.
